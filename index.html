    • Biography

    Shih-Chun Lin received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, USA. He received the Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and the Master of Science in Communications Engineering from the National Taiwan University, Taiwan.

    He is an Associate Professor and the Director of the Intelligent Wireless Networking (iWN) Laboratory in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the North Carolina State University. His research interests are in Wireless Software-Defined Networking systems (including B5G networks, Internet of Things, and cyber-physical systems), Intelligent Edge Computing architecture, Big-Data Analytic and Machine Learning, and Wireless Communications in Underground and Underwater environments.
